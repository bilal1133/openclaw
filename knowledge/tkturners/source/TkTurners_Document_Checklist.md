## 3. Client-Facing Commercial Documents

These are the documents you hand to US clients. They must be professional, clearly written, and governed by terms your clients will accept.

### 3a. Core Contract Stack

| # | Document | Priority | Notes |
|---|----------|----------|-------|
| 3.1 | **Master Services Agreement (MSA)** — the governing contract for all work with a client; covers liability, IP, payment terms, dispute resolution, governing law | 🔴 Critical | Every client signs this once; all SOWs sit under it |
| 3.2 | **Sprint SOW Template** — fixed-scope statement of work for the Integration Foundation Sprint; includes deliverables, timeline, acceptance criteria, milestones, payment schedule | 🔴 Critical | One of the most important documents in the business |
| 3.3 | **Retainer SOW Template** — monthly recurring services agreement; covers scope, KPI targets, reporting cadence, minimum term, renewal, and exit terms | 🔴 Critical | Governs all recurring retainer revenue |
| 3.4 | **Change Order / Amendment Template** — formally documents and prices any scope change during a sprint | 🔴 Critical | Prevents scope creep from destroying sprint margin |
| 3.5 | **Pilot Agreement Template** — special version of Sprint SOW for pilot clients; includes case study rights, testimonial rights, and reduced pricing rationale | 🔴 Critical | Needed before your first 3 pilot clients |

### 3b. Pre-Contract Documents

| # | Document | Priority | Notes |
|---|----------|----------|-------|
| 3.6 | **Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)** — protects both parties when sharing sensitive business data during discovery | 🔴 Critical | Always signed before discovery begins |
| 3.7 | **Proposal Template** — the document you send after discovery to summarise the solution, scope, timeline, and price | 🔴 Critical | Bridge between discovery call and signed SOW |
| 3.8 | **Letter of Engagement** — lightweight one-page summary of agreed work before full SOW is drafted | 🟡 Important | Useful for fast deal progression |

### 3c. Ongoing Client Documents

| # | Document | Priority | Notes |
|---|----------|----------|-------|
| 3.9 | **Data Processing Agreement (DPA)** — documents how TkTurners handles client data; increasingly required by US clients | 🟡 Important | Especially needed for clients in regulated industries |
| 3.10 | **Service Level Agreement (SLA) Schedule** — defines uptime, sync latency, error response, and escalation commitments | 🟡 Important | Usually attached to the Retainer SOW |
| 3.11 | **Partner Referral Agreement** — governs the referral fee, process, and confidentiality for ERP and agency partners | 🟡 Important | Needed before you formalise partner relationships |
| 3.12 | **White-Label Reseller Agreement (future)** — governs how partners sell TkTurners services under their own brand | 🟢 Build over time | Needed when productization begins in Year 2 |
