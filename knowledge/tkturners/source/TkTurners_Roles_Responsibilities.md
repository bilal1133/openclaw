## Leadership

---

### CEO / Founder

**Reports to:** Board / Stakeholders

**Role Purpose**
Owns the overall vision, strategy, and commercial outcomes of TkTurners. In Year 1, acts as the primary salesperson and the external face of the company with key clients and partners.

**Core Responsibilities**
- Sets the company vision, positioning, and 12–24 month strategic roadmap
- Leads and closes high-value client deals, particularly anchor sprint engagements
- Owns all key partnership relationships — ERP consultants, payment platforms, agencies
- Allocates resources, approves pricing exceptions, and makes final hiring decisions
- Chairs the weekly leadership operating review and monthly financial steering meeting
- Represents TkTurners at industry events, partner conversations, and case study publications
- Monitors leading indicators and course-corrects GTM and delivery strategy as needed
- Builds and maintains company culture, values, and team accountability standards

**Success Metrics**
- Total company revenue vs Base / Target / Stretch scenarios
- Number of qualified opportunities generated per month (target: 10+)
- Sprint close rate from qualified pipeline (target: 20%+)
- Partner referral pipeline contribution as % of total pipeline
- Team retention and satisfaction

**Key Skills & Qualifications**
- Deep expertise in ecommerce operations, ERP systems, or SaaS services
- Demonstrated ability to sell complex technical solutions to C-suite buyers
- Strong commercial judgment — pricing, scoping, and contract negotiation
- Executive communication and storytelling, both written and in-person
- Ability to context-switch between strategy, sales, and delivery oversight

---

### COO / Operations Lead

**Reports to:** CEO / Founder

**Role Purpose**
Translates the company strategy into efficient, scalable daily operations. Owns the internal operating model — from team structure and capacity management to financial reporting and process quality — so the CEO can focus on growth.

**Core Responsibilities**
- Designs and maintains TkTurners' operating model: sprint capacity, retainer scheduling, and team utilization
- Owns the internal financial model: tracking actuals vs scenarios, managing cost centers, and preparing monthly steering reports
- Manages vendor, contractor, and tooling relationships (subscriptions, SLAs, renewals)
- Leads hiring execution: sourcing, offer management, onboarding, and role scoping
- Enforces the weekly operating cadence and ensures all cross-functional meetings produce decisions and logged outputs
- Identifies and eliminates operational bottlenecks before they impact client delivery
- Owns legal administration: MSAs, SOW templates, NDAs, and compliance checkpoints
- Coordinates finance, admin, and HR functions in the early stage; transitions these to dedicated hires as the company scales

**Success Metrics**
- Team utilization rate (billable hours / available hours, target: 70%+)
- Gross margin after direct delivery cost (target: 65%+)
- Time-to-hire for open roles
- Operating cost vs budget by month
- Decision log completion rate from weekly operating rhythm

**Key Skills & Qualifications**
- Experience running operations in a professional services or SaaS environment
- Strong financial modeling and budget management capability
- Process design and documentation discipline
- Cross-functional leadership — comfortable managing engineering, sales, and finance simultaneously
- Contract and vendor management experience

## Delivery

---

### Delivery Manager / Program Manager

**Reports to:** COO / Operations Lead

**Role Purpose**
Owns the end-to-end execution quality of every client sprint and retainer engagement. Ensures projects are delivered on time, on scope, and at the quality standard required for client acceptance and case study proof.

**Core Responsibilities**
- Manages sprint timelines, milestones, and delivery risk logs across all active client engagements
- Runs daily standups and weekly delivery reviews with the engineering and automation team
- Enforces stage-gate rules: no automation deployment until data validation passes; no model rollout without 30 days of stable data
- Manages the change request process — identifies out-of-scope work, gets signed amendments, and protects delivery margin
- Coordinates client-facing communications during delivery: status updates, blocker escalations, and acceptance sign-off
- Owns quality assurance: reviews integration test logs, monitors error rates, and ensures SLA compliance before go-live
- Maintains and improves the sprint delivery playbook and acceptance criteria templates
- Tracks sprint delivery hours against estimates and flags overruns early

**Success Metrics**
- % of sprints delivered on time and within scope
- Change request frequency per sprint (lower is better)
- Client acceptance sign-off within agreed timeline
- Time-to-first-value per engagement (target: 45 days or less)
- Sprint margin vs estimate (delivery hours actual vs planned)

**Key Skills & Qualifications**
- 3+ years managing technical project delivery in ecommerce, SaaS, or systems integration
- Strong ability to hold scope boundaries while maintaining positive client relationships
- Familiarity with integration architectures (APIs, ERPs, payment gateways)
- Excellent written communication — status reports, risk logs, SOW reviews
- Experience with project tools (Linear, Notion, Jira, or equivalent)

## Engineering Team

---

### Solutions Architect

**Reports to:** Delivery Manager

**Role Purpose**
Bridges the gap between client pain and technical solution. Leads discovery, writes integration blueprints, scopes SOWs, and owns the technical strategy for every engagement before handing to the engineering team for execution.

**Core Responsibilities**
- Leads technical discovery sessions with client stakeholders to map current systems, data flows, and pain points
- Produces the Integration Blueprint: source-to-destination mapping, latency SLAs, error-handling protocols, and data governance notes
- Writes and reviews sprint SOWs with the Delivery Manager to ensure technical scope is accurate and bounded
- Advises the Automation Engineer on workflow design — which automations to build first based on the Automation Opportunity Scorecard
- Evaluates new client tech stacks to assess integration complexity and flag high-risk connectors before a deal is signed
- Maintains the reusable integration adapter library and contributes patterns after each new connector is built
- Participates in bi-weekly architecture reviews to share learning across the engineering team
- Supports presales where technical credibility is required to close a deal

**Success Metrics**
- Blueprint-to-SOW accuracy (% of sprints with change requests due to underscoped technical work)
- Reusable adapter adoption rate (% of new sprints using existing connector patterns)
- Time from discovery to signed SOW
- Client technical satisfaction at sprint close

**Key Skills & Qualifications**
- Deep knowledge of ERP systems (NetSuite, Acumatica, Sage, SAP) and their integration APIs
- Shopify, WooCommerce, or BigCommerce platform architecture experience
- Payment gateway integration knowledge (Stripe, Adyen, Braintree, PayPal)
- Ability to read and produce technical documentation at both business and engineering levels
- Strong discovery and consultative listening skills — comfortable leading workshops with non-technical buyers

---

### Integration Engineer

**Reports to:** Delivery Manager / Solutions Architect

**Role Purpose**
Builds and maintains the technical connectors that link ERP systems, storefronts, payment gateways, and sales channels. Responsible for data integrity, sync reliability, and error handling in all integration layers.

**Core Responsibilities**
- Implements integration blueprints as production-grade connectors using REST APIs, webhooks, file-based sync, or middleware platforms
- Maps data fields between source and destination systems — orders, inventory, customers, payments, and fulfillment events
- Writes and executes integration test plans to validate data accuracy, latency SLAs, and edge-case handling
- Deploys monitoring hooks and alerting so sync errors are caught before clients notice them
- Documents connector logic, retry policies, and escalation paths in the team runbook
- Builds and contributes to the reusable adapter library to reduce future sprint setup time
- Participates in code review and bi-weekly architecture sessions to maintain delivery consistency
- Supports post-launch monitoring during the first 30 days of a new integration

**Success Metrics**
- Integration error rate post-go-live (target: below agreed SLA threshold)
- Data sync latency vs committed SLA
- Connector build time vs blueprint estimate
- Runbook quality score (reviewed by Delivery Manager per sprint)

**Key Skills & Qualifications**
- Proficiency in Python, Node.js, or similar; REST API integration experience essential
- Hands-on experience connecting at least one major ERP to a commerce platform
- Understanding of JSON/XML data transformation, schema mapping, and idempotency patterns
- Familiarity with iPaaS tools (Celigo, Jitterbit, MuleSoft) is a plus
- Strong debugging discipline — systematic, documented, and reproducible

---

### Automation Engineer

**Reports to:** Delivery Manager / Solutions Architect

**Role Purpose**
Designs and deploys the rule-based and AI-assisted workflows that sit on top of the integrated data layer. Responsible for building the automation playbooks that directly drive client revenue outcomes — the core value of the Revenue Automation Retainer.

**Core Responsibilities**
- Translates the Automation Opportunity Scorecard into prioritized workflow implementations
- Builds automation triggers, conditional logic, and escalation paths across commerce, ERP, and payment data
- Develops failed payment retry sequences, inventory alert workflows, and order exception automations
- Configures and tunes AI voice agent workflows where applicable (abandoned checkout recovery, payment retry outreach)
- Maintains the Automation Scorecard dashboard for each retainer client — tracking workflow performance against baseline
- Runs the weekly Automation Optimization Review with the Delivery Manager to approve workflow changes
- Documents all automations in the client workflow playbook — logic, triggers, expected outcomes, and rollback steps
- Monitors automation performance continuously and proposes backlog additions during monthly retainer reviews

**Success Metrics**
- Automation uplift vs baseline per client (conversion rate, failed payment rate, AOV)
- Workflow uptime and trigger success rate
- Number of active automations per retainer client (growth over time)
- Time from automation concept to live deployment

**Key Skills & Qualifications**
- Experience with workflow automation tools (Zapier, Make, n8n, or custom orchestration)
- Understanding of ecommerce revenue levers — conversion, AOV, retention, payment recovery
- Familiarity with AI/ML APIs for integrating predictive outputs into workflow triggers
- Strong analytical mindset — able to interpret KPI data and design automations that respond to it
- Clear documentation habits; automation logic must be understandable to non-engineers

### Data Engineer / Analyst

**Reports to:** Delivery Manager / Solutions Architect

**Role Purpose**
Builds and maintains the normalized commerce event layer and KPI infrastructure that powers client dashboards, predictive jobs, and automation triggers. Data quality is the foundation of every TkTurners client outcome.

**Core Responsibilities**
- Designs and maintains the normalized commerce event model — orders, SKUs, payments, inventory, channel performance
- Builds lightweight data pipelines and transformation jobs that feed KPI dashboards and predictive models
- Runs daily data validation checks and produces the Wednesday KPI trend report for the leadership team
- Implements the predictive use cases: SKU demand forecasting, stockout risk warnings, failed payment prioritization, and channel mix recommendations
- Maintains model refresh cadence (weekly or bi-weekly) and monitors forecast accuracy against actuals
- Ensures human review is required before high-impact automation changes are triggered by model output
- Documents data models, source-of-truth mapping, and transformation logic in the client data dictionary
- Works closely with the Automation Engineer to expose clean model outputs as automation-ready triggers

**Success Metrics**
- Forecast accuracy across client SKU portfolios (target: improving trend toward 90%+)
- Data validation pass rate — daily checks (target: 99%+)
- Time from integrated data to first predictive output per new client
- KPI dashboard uptime and refresh reliability

**Key Skills & Qualifications**
- SQL proficiency essential; Python (pandas, dbt, or similar) strongly preferred
- Experience with data warehousing concepts and ETL/ELT pipeline design
- Understanding of ecommerce data structures — order tables, inventory ledgers, payment events
- Familiarity with forecasting methods (time series, moving averages, regression)
- Strong data quality instinct — finds anomalies before they cause downstream errors

### AI / ML Engineer

**Reports to:** Solutions Architect

**Role Purpose**
Owns the AI capability layer — building, tuning, and monitoring the models and agent integrations that power TkTurners' Predictive Intelligence Module and AI Voice Agent Module. Responsible for translating AI capability into measurable client outcomes.

**Core Responsibilities**
- Builds and maintains predictive models for demand forecasting, stockout risk, failed payment recovery, and channel mix optimization
- Integrates AI voice agent platforms into client workflows — configuring scripts, escalation logic, and A/B test frameworks
- Evaluates and recommends AI tools, APIs, and frameworks appropriate for each client use case
- Monitors model drift and retrains on updated data at agreed cadences
- Designs the Agent-Ready Commerce Architecture audit framework (agentic commerce readiness layer)
- Stays current with emerging protocols (MCP, A2A, AP2) and assesses their applicability to TkTurners client data layers
- Works with the Data Engineer to ensure model outputs are clean, versioned, and automation-ready
- Produces model performance reports for leadership and clients as part of the monthly optimization review

**Success Metrics**
- Predictive model accuracy improvements over time per client
- AI voice agent conversion rate vs baseline (target: measurable lift within 60 days)
- Model retraining cadence adherence
- Time to deploy new AI module per client engagement

**Key Skills & Qualifications**
- Hands-on experience with ML frameworks (scikit-learn, PyTorch, or equivalent)
- Experience integrating LLM APIs and AI agent platforms into production workflows
- Understanding of ecommerce data patterns and how to apply them to predictive problems
- Awareness of emerging agentic commerce protocols is a strong plus
- Ability to explain model outputs to non-technical client stakeholders

## Design and Brand

**Reports to:** CEO / COO

**Role Purpose**
Owns the visual identity and brand standards of TkTurners. Ensures that every client-facing asset — from pitch decks to case studies to the website — communicates credibility, clarity, and professionalism consistent with the company's niche positioning.

**Core Responsibilities**

**Key Skills & Qualifications**
- Proficiency in Figma, Adobe Creative Suite, or equivalent design tools

- Ability to translate complex technical concepts into clean, simple visual communication
- Experience designing pitch decks and sales materials that convert

**Reports to:** CEO / GTM Lead

**Role Purpose**
Turns pilot results, case studies, and thought leadership into a steady content engine that generates inbound interest and supports the outbound sales motion. Owns TkTurners' external presence and brand credibility in the ecommerce operations market.

**Core Responsibilities**

## Sales and Client Success

---

### GTM Lead (Head of Sales)

**Reports to:** CEO / Founder

**Role Purpose**
Owns the outbound pipeline, discovery call volume, and sprint close rate. Responsible for building the repeatable sales system — from ICP targeting and sequence management to proposal delivery and contract execution.

**Core Responsibilities**
- Manages the outbound prospecting system: list building, sequencing, personalization, and daily outreach volume (target: 160+ touches/month)
- Books and leads initial discovery calls; qualifies accounts against ICP criteria before advancing to the Solutions Architect
- Manages the CRM — pipeline stages, deal notes, next steps, and forecast accuracy
- Coordinates proposal delivery with the Solutions Architect — ensures technical scope matches buyer pain
- Runs the weekly pipeline and deal review with the Founder every Monday
- Tracks the sales funnel from outreach to close and reports on conversion rates at each stage
- Builds and maintains the partner referral channel — nurtures ERP consultant and agency relationships to generate warm leads
- Identifies trigger events (fundraising, leadership changes, new channel launches) that signal ICP urgency

**Success Metrics**
- Qualified opportunities generated per month (target: 10+)
- Sprint close rate from qualified pipeline (target: 20%+)
- Discovery calls held per month (target: 20+)
- Outbound touches per month (target: 160+)
- Time from first contact to signed SOW (cycle length trend)

**Key Skills & Qualifications**
- 3+ years in B2B technology or professional services sales — ideally ecommerce, ERP, or SaaS
- Outbound prospecting discipline: comfortable with cold email and LinkedIn outreach at volume
- Ability to qualify technical pain and map it to TkTurners' service ladder
- CRM proficiency (HubSpot, Salesforce, or equivalent)
- Strong verbal communication — discovery call presence and light consultative selling

### Client Success Manager

**Reports to:** COO / Delivery Manager

**Role Purpose**
Manages the ongoing relationship with retainer clients after the sprint closes. Ensures clients see and feel continuous value from the Revenue Automation Retainer — protecting renewal, driving upsell, and building the references that fuel future sales.

**Core Responsibilities**
- Owns the relationship with 8–15 active retainer clients — primary point of contact for questions, escalations, and monthly reviews
- Prepares and leads the monthly KPI review: presents automation performance, forecast accuracy, and recommended next actions
- Monitors KPI trends proactively — flags anomalies or opportunities before clients ask about them
- Identifies upsell moments: when a retainer client is ready for the Predictive Intelligence Module or AI Voice Agent Module
- Manages retainer renewals — proactively initiates renewal conversations 60 days before term end
- Collects client testimonials and coordinates case study participation with Marketing
- Serves as the early warning system for churn risk — escalates to Delivery Manager or Founder when satisfaction dips
- Runs the quarterly business review (QBR) with each retainer client — summarising outcomes and presenting roadmap proposals

**Success Metrics**
- Retainer renewal rate (target: 85%+)
- Retainer upsell rate — % of retainer clients who purchase add-on modules within 6 months
- Net Promoter Score (NPS) from retainer clients
- Client-reported KPI improvement vs baseline
- Number of case study participants and testimonials generated per quarter

**Key Skills & Qualifications**
- Experience in customer success, account management, or consulting in a technical services environment
- Ability to read and interpret ecommerce KPI data and explain it clearly to non-technical buyers
- Proactive relationship management — doesn't wait for clients to surface problems
- Negotiation and renewal conversation skills
- Strong empathy — able to represent the client's perspective internally and advocate for their needs

### Partnership Manager

**Reports to:** CEO / GTM Lead

**Role Purpose**
Builds and manages TkTurners' ecosystem of referral partners — ERP consultants, Shopify Plus agencies, payment specialists, and accounting firms. The partner channel is TkTurners' highest-leverage, lowest-cost lead generation engine at scale.

**Core Responsibilities**
- Identifies and initiates relationships with ERP consulting firms, payment specialists, and ecommerce agencies that serve the TkTurners ICP
- Develops the formal partner referral program: incentive structure, co-marketing agreements, and onboarding documentation
- Runs regular partner enablement sessions — ensuring partners understand TkTurners' ICP, offer ladder, and referral process
- Tracks partner-sourced pipeline and reports on referral volume, conversion rate, and revenue attribution monthly
- Manages co-marketing initiatives with partners: joint case studies, webinars, shared content, and event co-sponsorship
- Builds towards a white-label reseller model in Year 2 — packaging TkTurners' delivery artifacts for partner distribution
- Identifies and pursues technology partnerships (ERP vendors, Shopify Plus certification, payment platforms)
- Maintains the partner CRM and ensures follow-up commitments are honored

**Success Metrics**
- Number of active referring partners (target: 5+ by end of Year 1)
- Partner-sourced qualified opportunities per month
- Partner revenue contribution as % of total pipeline
- Partner satisfaction and engagement score (quarterly survey)
- Co-marketing assets produced per quarter

**Key Skills & Qualifications**
- Experience in channel sales, partner development, or business development in a technology environment
- Existing relationships in the ERP consulting, Shopify, or ecommerce agency ecosystem is a strong advantage
- Ability to design and manage a formal partner program from scratch
- Strong relationship management and follow-through discipline
- Commercial acumen — understands how to structure agreements that are mutually beneficial

## Admin, Finance and HR

---

### Finance and Admin Lead

**Reports to:** COO / Operations Lead

**Role Purpose**
Ensures TkTurners runs cleanly as a business — invoicing is accurate and on time, books are reconciled, payroll is processed, and the administrative burden is kept off the delivery and sales team so they can focus on clients.

**Core Responsibilities**
- Manages invoicing for all sprint milestones and monthly retainer billing — ensures payments are collected per contract terms
- Reconciles bank accounts and maintains accurate bookkeeping (QuickBooks, Xero, or equivalent)
- Processes payroll and contractor payments on schedule
- Tracks accounts receivable and follows up on late payments
- Prepares the monthly financial summary for the COO and Founder: actual vs budget, cash position, and forecast
- Manages vendor subscriptions, tool licenses, and recurring operating costs
- Handles employee administration: contracts, onboarding paperwork, benefits coordination, and compliance
- Supports the COO with legal administration — storing executed MSAs, SOWs, and NDAs in an organized system

**Success Metrics**
- Invoice-to-payment cycle time (days outstanding vs contract terms)
- Accounts receivable overdue balance (target: zero 60+ day overdue)
- Payroll processing accuracy and on-time rate
- Monthly financial close cycle time
- Operating cost tracking accuracy vs budget

**Key Skills & Qualifications**
- Bookkeeping and accounts receivable experience; accounting software proficiency (QuickBooks or Xero)
- Strong organizational discipline — nothing falls through the cracks
- Familiarity with contract administration and basic HR administration
- Discreet and trustworthy — handles confidential financial and personnel information
- Proactive communicator — flags financial risks before they become problems

### HR Lead / People Operations

**Reports to:** COO / Operations Lead

**Role Purpose**
Builds and protects the culture, people systems, and talent pipeline that TkTurners needs to scale. Responsible for hiring execution, onboarding quality, performance frameworks, and keeping the team engaged and high-performing.

**Core Responsibilities**
- Manages full-cycle recruitment: job descriptions, sourcing, screening, interview coordination, and offer management
- Designs and runs the onboarding program — ensuring new hires are productive and culturally integrated within 30 days
- Builds and administers the performance review framework and compensation benchmarking process
- Manages employee relations: addresses concerns, mediates conflicts, and supports manager effectiveness
- Tracks team utilization, morale, and retention risk — reports quarterly to the COO and Founder
- Administers benefits, leave policies, and compliance with employment law
- Develops the learning and development calendar — ensuring engineers and delivery staff stay current with ecommerce and AI tooling
- Supports the Founder in defining culture, values, and the company's employment brand

**Success Metrics**
- Time-to-hire for open roles (target: under 45 days for engineering, under 30 days for other roles)
- 90-day new hire retention rate
- Employee Net Promoter Score (eNPS) — measured quarterly
- Voluntary attrition rate (target: below 15% annually)
- L&D participation rate per quarter

**Key Skills & Qualifications**
- 3+ years in HR or people operations, ideally in a technology or professional services company
- Recruiting experience for technical roles (engineers, architects, data professionals)
- Strong interpersonal and conflict-resolution skills
- Familiarity with HR platforms (Gusto, Rippling, BambooHR, or equivalent)
- Understanding of employment law basics in the relevant jurisdiction

## Cross-Functional Specialists

---

### Technical Writer / Documentation Lead

**Reports to:** Delivery Manager

**Role Purpose**
Produces and maintains the documentation that allows TkTurners to scale delivery without key-person dependency. Every integration, automation, and client workflow must be documented so that any engineer can pick it up, and so that IP is preserved as the company moves toward productization.

**Core Responsibilities**
- Writes and maintains integration runbooks: connector logic, data mapping, retry policies, error types, and escalation contacts
- Documents automation playbooks: trigger conditions, workflow steps, expected outcomes, and rollback procedures
- Maintains the discovery checklist, SOW templates, and acceptance criteria templates in the delivery playbook
- Writes internal how-to guides and onboarding documentation for new engineers joining the delivery team
- Produces client-facing documentation: data dictionary, KPI definitions, and monthly report templates
- Manages the documentation platform (Notion, Confluence, or equivalent) — keeps it organized, version-controlled, and searchable
- Works with the Product Manager (when hired) to produce user-facing documentation for the client portal
- Reviews all technical deliverables for documentation completeness before sprint closure

**Success Metrics**
- Documentation coverage rate (% of connectors and automations with complete runbooks)
- Time for a new engineer to become productive using only documentation (measured at onboarding)
- Documentation staleness rate (% updated within last 30 days vs total)
- Delivery Manager satisfaction score with documentation quality per sprint

**Key Skills & Qualifications**
- Exceptional written communication — clear, structured, and appropriately technical
- Experience documenting APIs, integration architectures, or technical workflows
- Ability to interview engineers and extract tacit knowledge into written form
- Documentation platform management (Notion, Confluence, GitBook, or equivalent)
- Detail orientation — catches inconsistencies, gaps, and outdated information
